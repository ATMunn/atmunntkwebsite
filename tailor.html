The rules for Ribbons are set out in rule 2438. A quick guide to how most Ribbons work: various events cause you to "earn" a Ribbon; you then "qualify for" that Ribbon for 7 days. While you qualify for a Ribbon (and do not already own that Ribbon, and have not owned it recently), any player can "award" you that Ribbon. A few Ribbons work differently; see rule 2438 for details.
